Trump Tower Floor Plan During The Russian Meeting

A man discovers the donuts in the executive lounge are gone. He spends the morning searching the building directory for Hispanic names and anonymously reporting them to immigration.

23rd Floor, Room 11

A woman gives a presentation to colleagues, all of whom stare at phones Googling Ultimate Fighting Championship highlights and Instant Pot recipes.

12th Floor, Room 14


Three children cannot find their mother as people in business casual laugh and point at them.

5th Floor, Room 8

An Escape Room. Clues are discovered by whispering niceties to miniature golden statues of Donald Trump. Melania has been stuck inside for three weeks.

3rd Floor, Room 1

Building security interrogates a housekeeper. From massage tables, naked execs debate the logic of why a housekeeper would steal hundreds of donuts. They all agree she's guilty.

Executive Lounge


Two men sit at desks. They only communicate through hand puppets. A woman furiously searches job ads.

15th Floor, Room 2

A man repeatedly smashes his forehead into a paper towel dispenser. A second man gives himself a pep talk in the mirror. The washroom attendant is on the phone with her bookie.

17th Floor, Men's Room


People sit around a table spitting at a picture of Hillary Clinton. The table is covered with donuts. Each donut has one bite missing.

25th Floor, Secret Conference Room

A woman shouts at Chinese businessmen. A sign language interpreter translates. A dance interpreter pirouettes. It is unclear if this is an Off Broadway show or a leveraged buyout.

18th Floor, Conference Room

ICE agents shut down the lobby having received dozens of calls of immigrant sightings. In custody are three security officers and a pregnant tourist who just stopped to take a picture.

Lobby


A man, clutching an empty picture frame, weeping. A woman trying to snare a pigeon on the windowsill with her iPhone charger.

33rd Floor, Room 20

A dark room, a desk. On the corner of the desk, a copy of “Fire and Fury: Inside the Trump White House,” almost two years before publication. Nearby, a man chain-vapes.

22nd Floor, Room 14

An actual McDonald’s franchise. Only a few people know it exists, though a staff of 15 is expected to have the full menu hot and ready. Most food is cooked and thrown away.

7th Floor, Room 6

Producers, lawyers and Bruce Willis discuss a Die Hard set inside Trump Tower. All agree Trump will have a cameo, though the lawyers insist Trump shoot a terrorist. Bruce Willis is texting.

44th Floor, Room 22


A doomsday bunker well stocked with artillery, Diet Coke, and the entire Stormy Daniels oeuvre.

9th Floor, Room 5

A man and woman stare at the Hudson River as ships pass. On Spotify, Air Supply’s Two Less Lonely People. They meet eyes and know, for certain, this is the low point of their careers.

47th Floor, Room 11


Room without furniture. Chris Christie sitting on the floor, a KFC family meal laid out, awaiting a lunch guest.

35th Floor, Room 3




FBI sting operation.
